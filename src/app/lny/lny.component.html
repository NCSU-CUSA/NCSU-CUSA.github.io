CUSA's 2023 Lunar New Year Festival

What is CUSA's Lunar New Year Festival?

The Lunar New Year Festival is one of our biggest events of the year! We bring together over 120 people and various Asian organizations to celebrate the incoming new year by playing games, eating amazing food, and giving out awesome prizes.

Be prepared to have fun and make sure that you bring your friends to come celebrate the year of the rabbit with us!

We are selling hand-packaged jars full of goodies for $7 each this year. See more details below!

When and Where is the LNY Festival?

CUSA's 2023 LNY Festival is being held in the Coastal Ballroom at Talley Student Union from 3pm to 5pm on January 14th, 2023

How Do I Participate?

All you need to do is to fill out the RSVP linked here and shown below or just show up at the door on the day of the LNY Festival!

- For paying members, admission is FREE
- The admission for those who RSVP for the event is $5
- The admission day of the event is $7 so be sure to fill out the RSVP form

Raffle Prizes!

During the LNY Festival you can earn tickets by visiting booths and playing games to enter into a raffle to try and win one of these prizes!

Lucky Tree Lego Set
Moon Plushie
Rice Cooker
And More!

Lucky Jars!

This year we are selling hand-packaged jars full of goodies for $7 each! Each jar can be turned into a water bottle and will include a bracelet, bunny stickers, candy, and fortune cards. We will only have 48 jars for sale so be sure to get one before they run out!

To grab your lucky jar please fill out the order form linked here or shown below

Once you ordered your lucky jar(s) can pick up your items on Saturday, January 14th in Talley Student Union Coastal Ballroom during the Lunar New Year Festival from 3:00-5:00pm. If you cannot make it to the Lunar New Year Festival, we will figure out a date and location for you to pick up your jar(s).
