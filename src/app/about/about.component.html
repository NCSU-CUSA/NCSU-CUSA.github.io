About CUSA

The Chinese Undergraduate Student Association (CUSA) is a prominent cultural student organization at North Carolina State University. Our mission is to promote Chinese culture throughout North Carolina State University as well as the local community. With events every year, such as Lunar New Year, Game Night, Camping Trip, Hot Pot Night, and Food Bank, we strive to promote Chinese culture through both service and recreation.
