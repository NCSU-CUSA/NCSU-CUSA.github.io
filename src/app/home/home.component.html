Our Mission

CUSA's goal is to promote Chinese culture throughout North Carolina State University as well as the local community. With events every year such as Lunar New Year, Game Night, Camping Trip, Hot Pot Night, and Food Bank, we strive to promote Chinese culture through both service and recreation.
